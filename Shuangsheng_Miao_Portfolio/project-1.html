Building Game: In this game, you are invited to become an urban planner for some cities to construct their new landscapes. During the game, you will need to complete goal lists with various required indexes by using limited funds and other elements in specified rounds. There are totally three levels in this game, and they all have different conditions, elements, and requirements. You are a young urban planner with a big dream and great passion to one day build your own ideal city. This is the day! From your experience through the rounds, you will be invited to construct different landscapes through each round. Let’s begin on your new journey! (Each person can be each level.)

Level #1: Welcome to Happy Village! I am your town mayor. I will guide you and get you started on building your new village! The goal of this level shown below, reach each value to 100 (environment, employment, population) within 15 rounds, and you will win the game. (Be careful all value should not be below 0.)

Level #2: Welcome to Pallet Town. The scale of this town is in all average size. The town is still in development. You need to use your resources and money wisely to achieve the goal of the stage. The goal of this level shown below, reach each value to 200 (environment, education, health) within 25 rounds, and you will win the game. (Be careful all value should not be below 0.)

Level #3: Welcome to Evergreen City, a beautiful modern city waiting for you to develop. There are more funds for you to spend and more choices for you to make in creating great large-scaled city for your civilians to enjoy. The goal of this level shown below, reach each value to 300 (environment, education, health, emotion) within 35 rounds, and you will win the game. (Be careful all value should not be below 0.)

In this game, my group meet some problems. At the first, my group should consider the main game play for this game and we should make sure the game have what type of fun. We search lots of information for website and we decide to build a 2D and half game.In this game we add different games of fun and some challenges. For example, we will randomly giving some gold lands and give them some bad weather in the game. All of the elements will influence the game.
Second, we think build these 2D and half buildings are very difficult for us because none of us were better at using AI. For these buildings, we search some examples from website and build them by ourselves in the AI. These are some vision examples for our game.

For the coding part, we think the buildings collision are very difficult for us because each build will need different numbers of lands. For example, if players want to build a park, they need 4 lands. If players want to build a pub, they need to 2 lands. We also need to think about the collision if you want to add a building near to other buildings. We collect lots of numbers to know the datas and try the datas for many different times.
